Everything you need to know about LetsTalk

LetsTalk is a free platform where you can practice languages by joining live voice and text chat rooms with native speakers from around the world. No textbooks, just real conversations.

Yes! LetsTalk is completely free. Create rooms, join conversations, share media, and use all features at no cost.

Rooms use WebRTC for peer-to-peer audio and video. Join a room, click the microphone to start talking, and toggle your camera for video chat. You can also share your screen.

We support 18+ languages including English, Spanish, Japanese, French, Korean, German, Portuguese, Chinese, Hindi, Arabic, and more. You can also select "Other" for any language not listed.

Yes! Each room has built-in apps including YouTube player, Pomodoro timer, Todo list, Chess, Document viewer, and a collaborative Whiteboard — perfect for study groups and tutoring.

Owner — The room creator. Full control over settings, roles, and room management.
Co-owner — Promoted by the owner. Can share the room link and manage guests.
Guest — Default role for joiners. Can participate in chat, voice, and apps.

📋 Community Rules

  1. Be respectful. Treat everyone with kindness regardless of language level.
  2. No hate speech. Discrimination, harassment, or offensive content is not tolerated.
  3. Stay on topic. Keep conversations relevant to the room's language and purpose.
  4. No spam. Avoid flooding rooms with repetitive messages or promotions.
  5. Help others learn. Gently correct mistakes and encourage progress.
  6. Report issues. Flag inappropriate behavior to room owners or co-owners.
